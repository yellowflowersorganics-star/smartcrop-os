## Labor Tracking

### Clock In/Out System

**How It Works:**
1. Navigate to **Labor** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Clock In"** to start your shift
3. The system tracks your time in real-time
4. Click **"Clock Out"** when finished
5. Labor costs are calculated automatically

**Features:**
- Real-time shift duration tracking
- Live clock display with seconds
- Active shift indicator
- Automatic cost calculation based on hourly rate
- Break time tracking

**Clock In Fields:**
- Category (monitoring, maintenance, harvesting, etc.)
- Description (optional)
- GPS location (optional)
- Hourly rate (optional - for cost calculation)

**Clock Out Fields:**
- Break minutes
- Additional notes

### Manual Entry

For backdated or missed entries:

1. Click **"Manual Entry"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Work Date:** Date of work
   - **Clock In Time:** Start time
   - **Clock Out Time:** End time (optional for active shifts)
   - **Break Minutes:** Total break time
   - **Hourly Rate:** Pay rate for cost calculation
   - **Category:** Type of work
   - **Description:** What was done

3. Click **"Save Work Log"**

### Work Categories

- **Monitoring:** Daily checks and monitoring
- **Maintenance:** Equipment and facility maintenance
- **Harvesting:** Harvest activities
- **Inoculation:** Substrate inoculation
- **Cleaning:** Sanitization and cleaning
- **Packing:** Product packing
- **Delivery:** Delivery tasks
- **Administrative:** Office work
- **Other:** General tasks

### Labor Statistics

View at a glance:
- **Total Hours:** Cumulative work hours
- **Total Cost:** Total labor expenses
- **Active Shifts:** Currently working
- **Completed:** Finished shifts

### Work History

- View all your work logs
- Filter by status (active, completed, approved)
- See detailed breakdown per shift
- Track labor costs per entry

## Financial Ratios & Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**Profit Margin:**
- Formula: (Net Profit / Revenue) √ó 100
- Healthy Range: 20-30%
- Below 10%: Review costs and pricing

**Labor Cost Percentage:**
- Formula: (Labor Costs / Revenue) √ó 100
- Target: 20-30% of revenue
- Above 40%: Consider efficiency improvements

**Cost per Kilogram:**
- Formula: Total Costs / Total Yield (kg)
- Compare across batches
- Use for pricing decisions

**Break-Even Point:**
- Revenue needed to cover all costs
- Monitor monthly
- Adjust production accordingly

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**1. Labor Costs Not Calculating**
- Ensure hourly rate is set
- Check clock out time is recorded
- Verify break minutes

**2. Missing Expenses**
- Review all receipts regularly
- Set up recurring entries for regular costs
- Use manual entry for missed items

**3. Incorrect Profitability**
- Verify all costs are recorded
- Check all revenue is logged
- Ensure date ranges match

**4. Can't Clock Out**
- Check active shift exists
- Refresh page
- Use manual entry if needed
